Frequently Asked Questions

Is the island safe?

Violent crime is very rare on Taniti

There are increasingly reports of pickpocketing and other petty crimes.

Do the locals speak English?

Many younger Tanitians speak English.

Very little English is spoken in rural areas, especially by the older residents.

What voltage is used in Taniti?

Power outlets are 120 volts (like the United States).

What currency is used in Taniti?

Taniti uses the U.S. dollar, but many businesses will also accept euros and yen.

Several banks facilitate currency exchange, and many businesses accept major credit cards.

Are there any hospitals or clinics in Taniti?

There is one hospital and several clinics. The hospital has many multilingual employees.

What are the drinking laws in Taniti?

Alcohol is not to be served or sold between the hours of midnight and 9:00 in the morning.

The drinking age is 18 and is not strictly enforced.

What is open on holidays? What holidays are celebrated in Taniti?

Taniti enjoys a large number of national holidays

Many tourist attractions and restaurants are closed on holidays, so plan accordingly.
